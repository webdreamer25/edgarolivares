about me

I am originally from Arica, Chile. Where is Chile? Chile is actually in the west coast of South America and yes it is a spanish speaking country but enough about that lets get to the good stuff. My journey with Front-end Development began in 1998 when as a little boy discovered the beauty of the internet. I began backward engineering websites that at the time were doing some impressive things with "tables" and "ECMAScript". Created my first webpage before highschool using angelfire and geocities free webserver services and accessing it through the L337 Netzero free internet services.
